# Privacy Policy for Burned Cookies

Burned Cookies is a browser extension designed to automatically delete cookies that are not protected by the user's whitelist.

## Data Collection

Burned Cookies does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal data.

The extension processes cookie domains and tab URLs locally in the browser only for the purpose of deciding which cookies should be deleted or kept.

## Local Storage

Burned Cookies stores the user's whitelist locally using the browser's extension storage. The whitelist may include domain entries such as `example.com` or `*.example.com`.

This whitelist is not transmitted to any server.

## Cookies

Burned Cookies can read and delete browser cookies because this is required for its core purpose. The extension does not upload, copy, sell, or share cookie contents.

## Remote Code

Burned Cookies does not use remote code. All code is included in the extension package.

## Third Parties

Burned Cookies does not transfer user data to third parties.

## Contact

For questions or issues, please use the GitHub repository issue tracker.
